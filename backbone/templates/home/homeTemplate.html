Fight the Food Coma

In Fall of 2011 the New York City Department of Health and Mental Hygiene added map demonstrations to the ongoing “Pouring on the Pounds” campaign begun under Mayor Michael Bloomberg. The posters focused on specific distances between two points you would have to walk off in order to burn off the calories in various sugary drinks. In the same spirit we wanted to add an interactive depth to this demonstration for the New York Times’ Hack Day 2013.

The interactive map hosted on this site allows you to scan or enter a barcode for your provided food item and determine how far you would need to walk in order to burn off the calories. Places to walk to are provided from the New York Times Events API.
